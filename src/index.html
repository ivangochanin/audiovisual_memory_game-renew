GAME IN GENERAL

Player choose two cards (each card contains sound and image) and tries to find two of the same cards. Find your own way of playing the game by selecting different game settings.

MODE - INTERVAL

Game with two tones at the same time - interval. The root is tone C1.

Symbols:

  • ↑ & ↓ - Indicates the direction of the interval
  • M - Major interval
  • m - Minor interval
  • P - Perfect interval
  • A - Augmented interval
  • d - Diminished interval

  • Level 1

    C1-C2 / Intervals within major scale / 1 octave(up):

    intervals:

  • Level 2

    C1-C2 / Intervals within chromatic scale / 1 octave(up).

  • Level 3

    C0-C2 / Intervals within chromatic scale / 1 octave(up) + 1 octave(down).

MODE - CHORD

Game with three or more tones at the same time - chord. The root is tone C.

Symbols:

  • ▵ - Major chords
  • m – Minor chords
  • ° - Diminished chords
  • numbers - indicates inversion or chord tension
