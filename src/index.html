sound memory game

Players choose two cards (each card contains sound and picture), at the same time, and try to find two of the same cards. Find your own way of playing the game by selecting different game settings.

  • LEVEL 1 - C1 - C2 / Major scale / 1 octave
  • LEVEL 2 - C1 - C2 / Chromatic scale / 1 octave
  • LEVEL 3 - C1 - C3 / Chromatic scale / 2 octaves
  • LEVEL 4 - C0 - C3 / Chromatic scale / 3 octaves
